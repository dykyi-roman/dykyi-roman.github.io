OfficeAI

Desktop app that turns your AI agents into employees of a virtual isometric office. Install, open — see all your agents in real time. Zero changes to your workflow.

Version 0.6.0

Features Agents Download Extension Requirements

Features

Real-time Agent States

See when your agents think, write code, use tools, respond, or go idle. Color-coded bouncing balls indicate activity tier.

Idle Zone Roaming

Idle agents roam the office — visit the water cooler, kitchen, sofa, meeting room, or lounge area.

Speech Bubbles

Hover over an agent to see what it's currently working on — live preview of the agent's responses.

Agents Panel

Track all active agents at once — model, status, project, and session duration at a glance.

Browser Extension

Chrome extension tracks ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude.ai browser sessions as office employees too.

Zero Intrusion

Passively observes AI processes via OS process scanning and log file monitoring. No CLI modifications, no plugins, no configuration required.
